Sybil Attacks

  • In the context of advancing decentralisation through token distribution via airdrops, projects often encounter an unexpected challenge:


  • the unintentional concentration of tokens among sybil actors.
  • Despite gleaning insights from prior airdrop experiences, the ongoing struggle continues between protocols and those seeking to exploit the system. With the sybil actor population on the rise, there‘s a palpable threat to both decentralisation and the preservation of privacy.
